Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CblFlasher

What is CblFlasher?

CblFlasher is an advanced email campaign management platform that allows you to create, send, and track professional email campaigns. It offers various campaign types, customizable templates, and detailed analytics.

How do I get an authorization code?

You can get an authorization code by contacting our admin at +1 (706) 248-1811 or by clicking the "Get Code" button during registration. The authorization code is required to activate your account.

What subscription plans do you offer?

We offer three subscription plans: Basic (₦20,000/month), Pro (₦30,000/month), and Enterprise (₦50,000/month). Each plan comes with different features and capabilities to suit your needs.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

Is my data secure with CblFlasher?

Yes, we take security very seriously. All data is encrypted and stored securely. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information and ensure the privacy of your campaigns.
